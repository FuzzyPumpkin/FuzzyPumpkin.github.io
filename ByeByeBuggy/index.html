Bugs belong in nature, not in your home.

Buggy

At ByeByeBuggy, we believe that bugs are an important part of the ecosystem. Just not your home’s ecosystem. Our product provides a humane alternative to the traditional and deadly flyswatter. Using ByeByeBuggy, you can trap bugs, identify them, and then release them back into nature.

How it Works

Hover on Tap each bug to see details.

Catch Button

By pressing the button, bugs are instantly captured.

Bug Release

Smooth-release lever allows easy and stress-free return of bug to its natural environment.

Magnifier

Get a close look at the captive bug for easy identification.

Bug Container

Roomy containment compartment allows for safe capture of large insects.
