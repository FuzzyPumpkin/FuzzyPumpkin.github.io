Note: When I initially started working on the assignments for this certification, I treated each assignment as a standalone with its own concept. I set up a basic page for each to fulfill requirements with the thought that I would come back later and improve upon the design and usability (aka: prettify). When I began the third project and needed to include a nav bar, I realized it would be much better to create all projects as part of one parent mock-site with an overarching theme. For this reason, the first two assignments in their initial form are drastically different from their final state.
